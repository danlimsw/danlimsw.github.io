## Group culture and mentoring expectations

The group maintains a living internal document of expectations and mentoring practices (adapted from *Expectations* by [John Boothroyd](https://med.stanford.edu/boothroydlab), Stanford University, 2022 revision).

As PI, I will strive to:

- Foster an inclusive and supportive research environment.
- Collaborate actively on scientific problem-solving and project strategy.
- Provide clear, timely, and actionable feedback.
- Align mentorship with each member's long-term career goals.
- Respect personal boundaries and well-being.
- Ensure fair recognition and attribution of contributions.
